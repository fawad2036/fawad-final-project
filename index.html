About

Dodo Pizza is a Russian pizza delivery franchise founded in 2011 by Fyodor Ovchinnikov. The corporation was founded in Syktyvkar, Russia. Its headquarters are also in Syktyvkar, as well as in Moscow. As of April 2024, the company has 841 pizzerias in Russia and 92 in 17 other countries (Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, United Kingdom, Cyprus, Germany, Nigeria, Vietnam,[1] China, Estonia, Lithuania, Romania, Slovenia, Belarus, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, UAE and Poland[2]). It's the largest pizza chain in Russia and Kazakhstan and one of Europe's fastest-growing restaurant chains.[3]
